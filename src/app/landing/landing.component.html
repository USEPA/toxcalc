Multidirectional Human Health Risk Assessment Calculators

Total Dose Conversion Calculator Convert between concentration and dose. Calculator can be used for liquids in liquids (e.g. ethanol in water), solids in liquids (e.g. sugar in water) or solids in solids (e.g. sugar in flour). Inhalation Dose Calculators Convert ppm or % in air to mg/m³. Convert mg/m³ to dose in mg/kg body weight. Human Equivalent Dose Conversion Calculators Convert a dose in animals to a dose in humans. Relative Bioavailability Correction Calculates the modifying factor necessary when route to route extrapolation is required for the PDE calculation.
